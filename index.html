Mission & Objectives

The Bitew Foundation (TBF) is a secular organization dedicated to achieving socio-economic change in Ethiopia. In pursuit of this goal, TBF focuses on basic education for children, believing this to be a critical requisite for progress. In keeping with this focus, our volunteers are involved with and support projects that are secular and have education-related components. The objectives of this foundation are:

To provide basic education to the poor and underprivileged children in Ethiopia.

To encourage the formation of various local groups, both in Ethiopia and the United States, which will engage with larger sections of the population.

To support and cooperate with individuals and groups already engaged in similar activities.

To raise the required human and financial resources to achieve the objectives.

To provide opportunities for individuals based in the United States who wish to participate in TBF activities in Ethiopia.

To address, whenever possible, other issues affecting human life such as health care, economic development, human rights, environment, socio-economic issues and women’s issues.
